Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. How do you track revenue?

We use automated tracking systems integrated into your game to monitor Robux earnings transparently. You'll have access to real-time dashboards showing all revenue data.

Q2. When do I pay for custom development?

Payment is typically split into milestones: 30% upfront, 40% at mid-development, and 30% upon completion. We're flexible and can discuss terms that work for both parties.

Q3. Can I remove the revenue share later?

Revenue sharing is a permanent part of our agreement and cannot be removed. This model allows us to offer lower upfront costs and ongoing support for your game's success.

Q4. What if my game doesn't make money?

If your game doesn't generate revenue, you don't owe us anything beyond the initial development fee (for custom games). We only earn when you earn - that's our commitment to your success.
